# Water Treatment Process

Our production process includes:

1. Borehole Water Collection
2. Raw Water Storage
3. Sand Filtration
4. Activated Carbon Filtration
5. Water Softening
6. Micron Filtration
7. Reverse Osmosis (where applicable)
8. Ultraviolet (UV) Sterilization
9. Ozone Disinfection
10. Quality Testing
11. Automatic Packaging
12. Final Inspection
13. Distribution
